Common Questions

How is the paper delivered?

Handbill subscribers collect from designated locations. Home delivery subscribers receive their copy via trusted courier networks that operate outside the scrutiny of the Post Office.

Is my subscription confidential?

Absolutely. We keep no written records of subscriber names. Payment is accepted in coin, and our couriers know only routes, not identities.

Can I pay annually?

Yes. Annual subscribers to the Home Delivery edition pay 3s 6d (saving 6d). Benefactors pay £1 annually (saving 5d).

What if I move to a new parish?

Send word to your courier or write to our offices in Leeds. We will redirect delivery within a fortnight.

How can I contribute articles?

We welcome reports from workers in all trades. Submit unsigned letters to our Leeds office, or pass them through any of our known agents.
