How to use this guide

This design system serves two purposes:

  1. During design: Browse these pages to see how your token changes affect the entire system. Adjust colors, typography, and spacing—then check the components to see the results.
  2. After launch: Share this guide with your team as a living reference for brand standards and UI patterns.

Start by customizing the Brand section with your visual identity, then use the Components as building blocks for your pages.
