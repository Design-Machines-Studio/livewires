Clear space

Always maintain adequate clear space around the logo to ensure visibility and impact. The minimum clear space is defined by the height of the logomark.

The dashed line represents minimum clear space

Clear space rules

  • Minimum clear space equals the height of the logomark (the "x-height")
  • When possible, increase clear space for greater impact
  • Never place other elements within the clear space zone
